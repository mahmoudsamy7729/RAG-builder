CHATful branding asset placeholders

Add the following project-specific assets to public/ to match the metadata references in index.html:
- favicon.ico (multi-size favicon)
- chatful-favicon.svg (SVG favicon)
- chatful-logo.png (512x512 logo for web app icon)
- chatful-apple-touch-icon.png (180x180 for iOS)
- chatful-og.png (1200x630 social share image)

Once you add the files, the references in index.html will automatically pick them up.
